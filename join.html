Opportunities for students

Join AI-NE Lab

We welcome motivated undergraduate, M.Tech, and PhD students interested in neuroscience, biomedical engineering, artificial intelligence, mathematics, signal processing, and healthcare technology.

What you'll work on

Meaningful, cross-disciplinary projects

Students can contribute to projects involving neural data analysis, machine learning, brain-network modelling, epilepsy research, MEG/EEG studies, and computational neuroscience.

We particularly encourage students who are curious, willing to learn across disciplines, and motivated to work on meaningful questions at the intersection of engineering and brain health.

📊

Neural data analysis

EEG, MEG, and SEEG signal processing and connectivity.

🤖

Machine learning

Interpretable AI methods for biomedical and neural data.

🕸️

Brain-network modelling

Computational and dynamical models of brain activity.
