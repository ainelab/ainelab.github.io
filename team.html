Dr. Himanshu Kumar, Ph.D.

Assistant Professor · Mehta Family School of Biosciences and Biomedical Engineering, IIT Indore

Dr. Kumar specialises in biomedical signal processing, neurotechnology, and machine learning, with a focus on EEG/SEEG/MEG analysis, seizure characterisation, affective computing, and brain-network analysis. He was previously a Postdoctoral Fellow at Cleveland Clinic's Epilepsy Center and a Guest Scientist at University Hospital RWTH Aachen, Germany.

He earned his Ph.D. from IIT Madras (Applied Mechanics & Biomedical Engineering) and an Integrated M.Tech in Biomedical Engineering from IIIT Allahabad.
